Dadi Ke Nuskhe

Ghar ka paramparik gyaan, jab chahe poochho

Aao beta 🙏 Main hoon tumhari Dadi. Pet kharab ho, neend na aaye, ya bas yeh jaanna ho ki shahad kaise lena chahiye, mujhse poochho. Mere paas ghar ke aazmaye hue nuskhon ka poora pitara hai.
Yeh dadi-nani ke paramparik nuskhe hain, medical advice nahi. Gambhir ya lagatar takleef mein doctor se zaroor milein.
